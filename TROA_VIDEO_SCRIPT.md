## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Video Style:
- Modern, sleek transitions
- Purple-pink-orange gradient theme throughout
- Professional corporate style with energy
- Build anticipation and excitement

### Music Recommendations:
- Opening: Soft ambient (mysterious)
- Middle: Upbeat, inspiring (building)
- Cliffhanger: Dramatic crescendo
- Outro: Warm, inviting

### Text Animation Style:
- Fade in with slight zoom
- Smooth slide transitions
- Glitch effect for cliffhanger moment
- Professional typography (Sans-serif, bold)

### Color Palette:
- Primary: Purple (#9333EA)
- Secondary: Pink (#EC4899)
- Accent: Orange (#F97316)
- Background: White/Light gradients
- Text: Dark gray/Black for contrast

### Screenshot Usage:
1. `video_01_hero.png` - Hero section (Scenes 2, 12)
2. `video_02_features.png` - Features overview (Scene 2)
3. `video_03_committee.png` - Committee showcase (Scene 3)
4. `video_04_amenities.png` - Amenities showcase (Scene 4)
5. `video_05_gallery.png` - Instagram feed (Scene 5)
6. `video_06_helpdesk.png` - Payment integration (Scene 6)
7. `video_07_mobile.png` - Mobile responsive (Scene 7)
8. `video_08_feedback_banner.png` - Modern interface (Scene 2)

### Tips for Video Creation:
1. Use Ken Burns effect (slow zoom) on screenshots
2. Add subtle parallax effects
3. Use motion graphics for text animations
4. Keep each scene crisp and fast-paced
5. Build energy throughout the video
6. Make the "2026" reveal dramatic and memorable
7. End on a professional, inviting note
